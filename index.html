🚀 About the Protagonist of 「青春との戦い」

Eddy Luo (罗威迪 & 一ノ瀬 エイジ), an incoming PhD student at University of Georgia advised by Prof.Xiang Zhen. Before his PhD journey, he had a research internship on AI safety at Prof.Chaowei Xiao's Lab at University of Wisconsin-Madison. In pursuit of academic excellence, he has served as a research assistant at OSU NLP Group and ICICLE Institute under the mentorship of Prof.Yu Su. This valuable experience has significantly contributed to his understanding of natural language processing and multimodalities.

His CV is here: Eddy's Curriculum Vitae. Eddy welcomes collaboration opportunities. Please feel free to contact him at Email: luo.1455[shift+2]uga[dot]edu. どうぞよろしくお願いします!

Eddy's research interests:

Trustworthy AI & AI Safety

Using interpretability methods, discover security vulnerabilities in AI systems, including foundation models and AI agents, and develop corresponding defense and detection algorithms, including safety alignment strategies.

AI in Security

Leverage AI to drive defense and attack strategies on systems, including web system and operating system.

Lifelong AI Algorithms

Develop lifelong learning AI frameworks and defense systems by utilizing reinforcement learning, cognitive science, bio-inspired algorithms, active learning, and so on.
